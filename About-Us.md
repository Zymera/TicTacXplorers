## Welcome to the Xploration

Welcome to **TicTacXplorers**, where the classic game of strategy meets modern mobile gaming. 

TicTacXplorers is developed and published by **Zymera Studios**, an independent game development studio proudly operating out of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. What started as a passion project to revitalize the timeless game of Tic-Tac-Toe has evolved into a feature-rich experience complete with progression systems, virtual rewards, and competitive gameplay.

## Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create engaging, polished, and accessible games that respect the player. 

The mobile gaming market is crowded, but we believe there is always room for high-quality, thoughtfully designed games. With TicTacXplorers, we set out to build an environment where players can test their logic, earn XP, collect Stars, and unlock beautiful themes without compromising on performance or privacy.

## The Indie Developer Difference

As an independent studio, **Zymera Studios** is not driven by corporate mandates. We are driven by a genuine love for game design and our player community. This means:

* **Player Privacy First:** We collect only the data absolutely necessary to run the game, save your progress, and support our development through responsible advertising. 
* **Fair Monetization:** Our In-App Purchases are designed to enhance your experience, not gatekeep the core fun of the game. We are transparent about what you are buying.
* **Continuous Improvement:** We actively monitor bugs, crashes, and player feedback to continuously refine and expand the TicTacXplorers universe.
