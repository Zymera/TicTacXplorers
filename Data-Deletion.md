## 1. Your Right to Data Deletion

At **Zymera Studios**, we respect your privacy and your right to control your digital footprint. This Data Deletion Policy outlines the specific steps you can take to permanently remove your account and associated personal data from **TicTacXplorers** ("the App").

## 2. How to Request Data Deletion

We have built a dedicated, secure data deletion mechanism directly into the App. To initiate a deletion request, please follow these steps:

1. Open the App and navigate to the **Settings** menu.
2. Tap on the **Account** section.
3. Select **Request Data Deletion**.
4. Provide the requested confirmation details (such as an email address to receive your deletion confirmation) and submit the form.

## 3. Data Deletion Scopes

When submitting your request, you can choose exactly what data you wish to remove. We offer three deletion scopes:

* **All Data:** This is a complete purge. We will delete your Firebase Authentication UID, your core user document in our database, and all associated subcollections (including game history and economy balances).
* **Game Data Only:** This resets your progression. We will delete your specific game subcollections (economy balances, XP, and game logs), but your core account identity remains active.
* **Account Only:** This removes your Firebase Authentication UID, revoking your ability to log in with that specific identifier.

## 4. Processing Timeline

Because we verify requests to prevent accidental or malicious account deletions, the deletion process is currently handled manually by our team. 

Upon receiving your request, we commit to fully processing the deletion of your data within **7 working days**. You will receive a confirmation email once the process has been successfully completed.

## 5. Data Retained for Security and Analytics

While we respect your right to deletion, we must maintain the integrity and security of the App ecosystem. Even after a full deletion request is processed, the following non-personally identifiable information is retained:

* **Device Fingerprint / Security Hash:** An anonymized cryptographic hash of your device identifier is retained indefinitely. This cannot be used to identify you, but it is necessary to prevent banned users from creating new accounts, combat fraud, and maintain fair play. This practice is legally grounded in legitimate interest (e.g., GDPR Article 17(3)(b)).
* **Aggregated Analytics:** Telemetry and crash logs previously collected via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics are fully anonymized and aggregated. Because this data can no longer be attributed to your specific account, it will remain in our systems to help us balance the game and fix bugs.

## 6. Manual Requests via Email

If you no longer have access to the App or your device, you may also request data deletion by emailing us directly at **zymerastudios@gmail.com**. 

Please note that for emailed requests, you must provide your exact Firebase UID (if known) or the exact details of your account to allow us to verify ownership before proceeding with the deletion.
