## 1. Overview and Platform Governance

Thank you for playing **TicTacXplorers** ("the App"), developed by **Zymera Studios** ("we," "us," or "our"). 

All financial transactions and In-App Purchases (IAP) made within the App are processed exclusively by **Google Play Billing**. Because we do not process payments directly, all purchases are subject to Google Play's standard refund policies. This document serves as our supplemental policy outlining the specific conditions under which we will approve or deny manual refund requests.

## 2. Consumable Digital Goods (Virtual Currency)

"Consumable" items include virtual currency such as "Stars," "XplorerCoins," or "XP" packs.

* **General Rule:** Once a consumable item has been purchased and delivered to your account, it is **non-refundable**.
* **Usage:** If any portion of the purchased virtual currency has been spent or consumed within the game, the purchase is strictly ineligible for a refund.
* **Google Auto-Refund:** Google Play may allow you to request an automatic refund within the first 2 hours of purchase directly through the Play Store.

## 3. Non-Consumable Digital Goods (Features & Aesthetics)

"Non-consumable" items include one-time unlocks such as "Remove Ads" or cosmetic "Themes."

* **Satisfaction Window:** If you are unsatisfied with a non-consumable purchase, we will honor refund requests made within **48 hours** of the purchase date.
* **Revocation:** If a refund is granted for a non-consumable item, access to that feature or item (e.g., ad-free gameplay or the specific theme) will be immediately revoked from your account.

## 4. Subscriptions

If the App offers recurring subscriptions (e.g., premium memberships):
* **Cancellations:** You may cancel a subscription at any time via your Google Play Subscription Settings. Canceling prevents future billing.
* **Partial Periods:** We do not provide pro-rated refunds for partially used billing periods. You will retain access to the subscription features until the end of your current billing cycle.
* **First-Time Exception:** We allow a one-time refund for first-time subscribers if the request is made within **3 days** (72 hours) of the initial subscription charge.

## 5. Exceptions and Technical Failures

We reserve the right to grant exceptions to this policy in the following scenarios:
* **Technical Failure:** If a bug, server outage, or application error prevents you from receiving the item you purchased.
* **Accidental/Duplicate Purchase:** If our system registers a duplicate transaction for the exact same item in a short timeframe.

## 6. How to Request a Refund

1. **Primary Method (Google Play):** The fastest way to receive a refund is to request it directly through your Google Play account purchase history within the first 48 hours.
2. **Secondary Method (Support Email):** If you fall outside Google's automatic window but within our policy terms, you may email us at **zymerastudios@gmail.com**. 
   * You **must** include your Google Play Order Number (starts with GPA.) and your Firebase UID (found in the App's settings) in your request.
   * Please allow 7-15 business days for manual review.

## 7. Abuse of Policy

We monitor refund requests to prevent abuse. If we determine that an account is systematically purchasing and requesting refunds for digital goods, we reserve the right to deny the request and permanently ban the user account from the App.

## 8. Limitation of Liability

Refunds issued under this policy represent your sole and exclusive remedy regarding In-App Purchases. Under no circumstances will our refund liability exceed the actual monetary amount paid for the specific item in question.
