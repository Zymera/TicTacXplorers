## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **TicTacXplorers** ("the App"). The App is developed, published, and maintained by an individual developer operating under the commercial brand name **Zymera Studios** ("we," "our," or "us"). 

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this Privacy Policy. This document details our surgical approach to data collection, usage, and protection, ensuring compliance with global standards including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act).

**By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.**

## 2. Information We Collect

To operate the App, provide In-App Purchases (IAP), and serve advertisements, we collect the minimum necessary data. We do **not** collect personally identifiable information (such as your real name, physical address, or email) unless you explicitly provide it when contacting our support team.

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Data
* **Anonymous Device Identifiers:** A secure Firebase Unique Identifier (UID) generated via anonymous authentication to securely save your game progress.
* **Advertising ID:** Your Android Advertising ID (AAID), used strictly by our advertising partners to serve ads.
* **Device Information:** Data including your device model, operating system version, screen size, and language settings to ensure optimal App performance.
* **Network Data:** Your IP Address, utilized strictly for approximate geographical location (country/state level, never precise GPS location) to serve regionally appropriate advertisements and prevent fraud.

### 2.2 Game and Usage Data
* **Game State:** Your scores, experience points (XP), virtual currency balance (Stars), and historical gameplay data.
* **Telemetry and Interactions:** Anonymous data regarding how you interact with the App (e.g., button taps, session lengths, screens visited) collected via Firebase Analytics.
* **Diagnostic Data:** Crash logs, stack traces, and performance metrics collected via Firebase Crashlytics to identify and resolve bugs.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data strictly for the following purposes:
1. **Service Delivery:** To save your game state, process In-App Purchases via Google Play, and maintain your virtual inventory.
2. **Monetization:** To display advertisements through Google AdMob, allowing us to offer the App for free.
3. **Analytics and Improvement:** To analyze user behavior, fix technical issues, and improve gameplay mechanics.
4. **Security and Fraud Prevention:** To protect against cheating, unauthorized modifications, and abuse of our systems using device fingerprinting (secure hashing).

## 4. Third-Party Service Providers

We operate as an individual developer and rely on enterprise-grade third-party infrastructure. We share data solely with these trusted partners; **we do not sell your personal data.**

* **Google Firebase:** Used for anonymous authentication, cloud database hosting (Firestore), analytics, and crash reporting.
* **Google AdMob:** Used to serve advertisements within the App.
* **Google Play Billing:** Used to securely process all In-App Purchases. **Note:** We never see, collect, or store your credit card or financial information. All transactions are handled securely by Google.

For detailed information on how Google processes data, please refer to the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & Global Standards)

TicTacXplorers is rated for a general audience (13+). However, we implement strict, proactive safeguards to protect all users, including minors:

* **No Knowing Collection:** We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).
* **Non-Personalized Advertising Fallback:** We have proactively configured our ad networks (AdMob) to serve **non-personalized ads** to users flagged as under the age of consent, preventing behavioral tracking.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can locate and destroy that data.

## 6. Your Global Privacy Rights

### 6.1 European Economic Area (GDPR)
If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, restrict, or erase your data. 
* **Lawful Basis:** Our processing of game data is based on *Legitimate Interest* (operating the App). Processing for analytics and personalized advertising is based on your *Consent*. Fraud prevention processing is conducted under Article 17(3)(b).

### 6.2 United States (CCPA/CPRA)
California residents have the right to request disclosure of data collection practices and request data deletion. **We explicitly confirm that we do not sell your personal data.**

### 6.3 India (DPDP Act)
As a Data Fiduciary operating from India, we ensure your data is protected using industry-standard encryption. You have the right to request data correction and erasure.

## 7. Data Retention and In-App Deletion

Your data is retained only as long as you use the App. You have the absolute right to delete your data.

* **How to Delete:** Navigate to **Settings > Account > Request Data Deletion** within the App.
* **Processing:** Upon receiving a request, your Firebase Auth UID and all associated Firestore data will be manually purged within **7 working days**.
* **Exceptions:** Anonymized analytics data (which cannot identify you) and secure fraud-prevention hashes may be retained to protect the integrity of the App.
